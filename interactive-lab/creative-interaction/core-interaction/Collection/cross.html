Osaka based metalcore/EDM hybrid aficionados Crossfaith stomped their way across the UK a fews back with a main stage slot at Slam Dunk Festival, and now they’ve got a new EP on the way. The 3-track offering, ‘Freedom’, is scheduled for release on August 4th 2017 via UNFD. The new ‘Freedom’ EP, which features guest appearances from both Rou Reynolds of Enter Shikari and Japanese rapper Jesse, and acts as a buffer between their 2015 LP ‘Xeno’ and their yet-to-be-titled fifth studio album, which is set to drop in 2018.
