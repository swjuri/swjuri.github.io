Agency Resort Client Tim & Puma Mimi

Despite the LP's title "Don't Mess Around With Tim & Puma Mimi", this cover artwork encourages you to do just that: By turning the LP disc inside the sleeve, Tim and Puma Mimi's faces get swapped. Due to this intervention, the cover serves also as an analogy to the remixed tracks on the LP. The typeface used was custom made for this project.

[Designed by Agency Resort Client Tim & Puma Mimi]
