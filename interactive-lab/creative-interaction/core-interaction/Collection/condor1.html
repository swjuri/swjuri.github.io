The concept: The album was named Patagonia. There was a true story which sounded more like a myth, that in the land of Patagonia lived giants, who disappeared a long time ago. Inspired by this we created a concept that Patagonia is a past from the upcoming future perspective and the band members acting like they were strange creatures from the past and left their faces in the mold like fossils. Strangeness you can find in cover hand, which has six fingers. (will you notice?:) The process: The process was long and really challenging. We worked with a special odontology material, where we kept musicians faces for some time and made a real-time mask. After that, we poured out forms with gypsum. Also, we molded hands, noses and other body parts All creative process included CD packaging, photoshoot material, conceptual looking and hand souvenir which was made from the cover hand visualization.
