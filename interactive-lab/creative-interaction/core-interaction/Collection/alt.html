With his ability to design custom-made characters, New York designer Virgilio Tzaj has regularly used his talent to create album art. Today it's the rock band Alt-J who turned to him to design the cover of his latest album "This is All Yours". On the cover he imagines abstract and colorful shapes. The typographic part on the back, offers color schemes and evolves when the vinyl is in the case.
